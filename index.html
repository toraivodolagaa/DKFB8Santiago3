Ham Cheese and Avocado French Toast

Description

Who says french toast needs to be sweet? This savory french toast is tasty, nutritious, and so easy to make and can be done in the air fryer in under 15 minutes. Ingredients 2 servings 4 slices bread 2 slices cheese or to taste (I used colby jack cheese) 2 slices ham or to taste 1 avocado pitted and cut into thin slices 2 tbsp pickled jalapeno chopped (optional) 2 tbsp mayonnaise 2 eggs

Cooking

1

Crack two eggs into a shallow plate and whisk for about 10 seconds. Set aside. Line the fryer basket with grill mat or a sheet of lightly greased aluminum foil.

2

Spread some mayonnaise to one side of each slice of bread. Then, assemble the sandwich by putting ham, cheese, jalapeno and avocado in between two slices of bread.

3

Dip the both sides of the sandwich in egg mixture, each side for about 3 seconds. Put the sandwiches inside the fryer basket.
